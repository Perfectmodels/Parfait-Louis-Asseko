## 2025-05-02 - Ensure consistent language localization in ARIA labels
**Learning:** This application's UI strings and standard accessibility attributes (e.g., `aria-label`) are heavily localized in French (e.g., "Fermer la notification", "Retour en haut").
**Action:** Always write new ARIA labels and screen reader texts in French to match the target audience and existing codebase conventions, avoiding English fallbacks.
